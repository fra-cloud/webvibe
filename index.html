About

A cyborg form of life, Francesco Biasi was released in 1988 as an evolving software - hardware system. Since then, FB enjoys reading, playing video games, and connecting with other life forms. Thanks to an early 2010's update, art is now a core program functionality of FB's software. Main interests : phenomenology, interconnetion, storytelling and technical images.
